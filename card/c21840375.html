Hidden Spellbook

Trap/Normal

- Archetype: Spellbook

During your Turn: Target 2 Spell Cards in your Graveyard; shuffle those targets into the Deck.

Spellbook Library of the Heliosphere
Archetype: Spellbook
[Spell/Normal] NORMAL
If you have 5 or more "Spellbook" Spell Cards in your Graveyard: Excavate the top 2 cards of your Deck, add any excavated "Spellbook" Spell Cards to your hand, also shuffle the other cards into the Deck. You can only activate 1 "Spellbook Library of the Heliosphere" per turn. You cannot activate any Spell Cards the turn you activate this card, except "Spellbook" Spell Cards.
Spellbook of the Master
Archetype: Spellbook
[Spell/Normal] NORMAL
If you control a Spellcaster-Type monster: You can reveal 1 other "Spellbook" card in your hand, then target 1 "Spellbook" Normal Spell Card in your Graveyard, except "Spellbook of the Master"; this card's effect becomes that target's effect. You can only activate 1 "Spellbook of the Master" per turn.
