Ojama Country

Spell/Field

- Archetype: Ojama

Once per turn: You can send 1 "Ojama" card from your hand to the GY, then target 1 "Ojama" monster in your GY; Special Summon it. While you control an "Ojama" monster, switch the original ATK/DEF of all monsters on the field.

* The above text is unofficial and describes the card's functionality in the OCG.
