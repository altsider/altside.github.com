Sylvan Charity

Spell/Normal

- Archetype: Sylvan

Draw 3 cards, then, if you have a "Sylvan" card in your hand, reveal 2 cards from your hand including at least 1 "Sylvan" card, then place them on the top of the Deck in any order.
