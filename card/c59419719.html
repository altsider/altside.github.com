Fossil Fusion

[Spell/Normal] NORMAL

Fusion Summon 1 "Fossil" Fusion Monster from your Extra Deck, by banishing Fusion Materials mentioned on it from any GYs (except for materials with a specific GY requirement listed). If you banished monsters from both GYs, neither player can target that Special Summoned monster with monster effects. If a face-up "Fossil" Fusion Monster(s) you control is destroyed by battle or card effect, while this card is in your GY: You can add this card from the GY to your hand. You can only use this effect of "Fossil Fusion" once per turn.
