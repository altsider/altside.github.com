Branded Disciple

Spell/Continuous

- Archetype: Branded

When a monster effect activated by your opponent resolves while that opponent's monster has 0 ATK and/or DEF (its original ATK/DEF, if no longer face-up on the field), and you control a Level 8 or higher Fiend Fusion Monster, you can negate that activated effect. At the end of the Damage Step, if monsters with 0 ATK battled each other: You can destroy your opponent's battling monster. You can only use each effect of "Branded Disciple" once per turn.

* The above text is unofficial and describes the card's functionality in the OCG.
