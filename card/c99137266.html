Swordsoul Strife

Trap/Continuous

- Archetype: Swordsoul

If a card(s) is banished (except during the Damage Step): You can banish 1 Wyrm Synchro Monster or 1 "Swordsoul" card from your hand, Deck, GY, or that is face-up on either field. If this card is banished: You can Special Summon 1 "Swordsoul Token" (Wyrm/Tuner/WATER/Level 4/ATK 0/DEF 0). While that Token is in the Monster Zone, the player who Summoned it cannot Special Summon monsters from the Extra Deck, except Synchro Monsters. You can only use each effect of "Swordsoul Strife" once per turn.
