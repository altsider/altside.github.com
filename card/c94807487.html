Rising Sun Slash

Spell/Equip

Equip only to a "Utopia" monster. It cannot be destroyed by card effects. Each time an attack is negated, place 1 Rising Sun Counter on this card. The equipped monster gains 500 ATK for each Rising Sun Counter on this card. If the equipped monster you control would detach its material to activate its effect, you can treat this card as 1 of the materials.
