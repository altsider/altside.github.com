Cyberload Fusion

[Spell/Quick-Play] QUICK-PLAY

Fusion Summon 1 Fusion Monster from your Extra Deck, by shuffling the Fusion Materials listed on it into the Deck, including a "Cyber Dragon" monster that it lists as material, from among the monsters on your field and/or your face-up banished cards, but monsters you control cannot attack for the rest of this turn, except that Fusion Summoned monster. You can only activate 1 "Cyberload Fusion" per turn.
