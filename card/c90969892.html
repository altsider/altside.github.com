Tribe Drive

[Trap/Normal] NORMAL

If you have 3 or more different Monster Types on your field: You can choose 3 monsters from your Deck with different Types from each other but with the same Types as the monsters you control, then, your opponent randomly chooses 1 for you to either add to your hand or Special Summon, also place the remaining monsters on the bottom of your Deck in any order. You can only activate 1 "Tribe Drive" per turn.
